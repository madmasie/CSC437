Maddie's Garlic Parm Pasta

Quick, creamy, and garlicky dinner

  • Serving Size: 1 serving

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup ditalini pasta
  • 1 cup bone broth
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan
  • 1/2 summer sausage
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • pinch of salt
  • pinch of black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

Cooking Techniques Used

  • Chopping
  • Pan-frying
  • Roasting garlic
  • Toasting pasta
  • Simmering
  • Tossing

Ingredient Substitution Notes

  • Can sub sausage for any other meat or simply don't include sausage.

Instructions

  1. Chop the summer sausage and cook it in a frying pan.
  2. Once cooked, set the sausage aside.
  3. In the same pan, add the olive oil.
  4. Mince the garlic and cook it in the heated pan until roasted.
  5. Add the ditalini pasta and toast it for a few minutes.
  6. Add the bone broth and cook until most of the broth is absorbed by the pasta.
  7. Add the grated parmesan and stir until it forms a creamy sauce.
  8. Season with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  9. Add the cooked summer sausage back into the pan.
  10. Toss everything together and serve.
