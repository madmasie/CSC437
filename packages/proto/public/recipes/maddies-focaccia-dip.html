Maddie's Focaccia Dip

A simple, savory dipping oil perfect alongside fresh focaccia.

Recipe Details

  • Cooking Type: No-cook / Condiment
  • Difficulty Level: Easy
  • Time: 5 minutes
  • Serving Size: 1 serving

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 pinch of Kinder's Garlic All Purpose Seasoning
  • 1/2 tablespoon balsamic vinaigrette
  • 1 clove garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parmesan, shredded

Instructions

  1. Pour the olive oil into a small dipping dish.
  2. Add the balsamic vinaigrette and stir to combine.
  3. Add the chopped garlic, salt, and Kinder's Garlic All Purpose Seasoning.
  4. Top with shredded fresh parmesan.
  5. Serve immediately alongside fresh focaccia.
